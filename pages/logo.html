MEET SPROUT!

Sprout serves as the face of our web app. He's both the logo for our web app and the mascot. Sprouts design comes with many different variations depending on where he's placed on a website, or the web app. The preferred approach is to present sprout with a dark background and a border to make him stand out since he has a relatively light colour scheme.

Take a look at the variations

Black and White

Mono Coloured

Fully Coloured

Why did we choose this design?

When designing the look of Sprout, we wanted to go for a friendly look with a light hearted tone. Sprout is the face of our web app, and he is going to be the first thing the users will see when looking up our app. Since this we are a vegan/vegetarian recipe app, we wanted Sprout to reflect that using green and earthy colour tones.
