Quiz Buttons

  • colour scheme, font size, and indenting are cohesive throughout the interface
  • button background darkens and highlights border when clicked

Navigation Buttons

Navigation Overlay panel is accessable from the hamburger menu
located in the top right of the navigation bar.

  • Overlay links highlight when hovered
  • Overlay sticks to the top right of the interface
  • Forward and back buttons are present on all pages of the application.
  • Buttons darkens when hovering.
